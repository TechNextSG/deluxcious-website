Getting Here

Directions from Key Landmarks

On Foot

From E&O Hotel

Walk east along Lebuh Farquhar towards the waterfront. Turn right onto Jalan Sultan Ahmad Shah. Deluxcious is on the left after 200m — look for the restored green-shuttered shophouse facade.

5 minutes
By Grab / Taxi

From Georgetown Ferry Terminal

Take a Grab or taxi along Lebuh Light heading north. It becomes Jalan Sultan Ahmad Shah. The fare is typically RM6–8. Drop-off directly in front of the building — tell the driver the address or show them "15-17A Jalan Sultan Ahmad Shah."

8–12 minutes
By Car

From Penang Bridge

Take the Penang Bridge (Jambatan Pulau Pinang) and follow the expressway into George Town. Exit at Lebuh Chulia and head northwest towards the seafront. Turn left onto Jalan Sultan Ahmad Shah. Nearest parking: KOMTAR (10 min walk) or Gurney Plaza valet (5 min drive).

25–35 minutes from bridge toll
From Airport

Penang International Airport

Grab or airport taxi to the hotel takes approximately 25–35 minutes depending on traffic. We also offer private airport transfers — please contact us at least 24 hours in advance to arrange. Fare: approximately RM50–70 by metered taxi.

25–35 minutes
Bus

Rapid Penang Bus

Rapid Penang Route 101 (Komtar–Teluk Bahang) stops at the E&O Hotel, a 5-minute walk from us. Route 103 serves the Weld Quay area. The CAT (free city bus) runs along Pengkalan Weld and covers most heritage sites.

Varies
On Foot

Walking from Nearby

If you are already in George Town, WhatsApp us your starting point and we will send you a walking pin. The restaurant is within easy reach of most heritage zone hotels and guesthouses.
