01 · Signature Experience

Deluxcious Spa

The Deluxcious Spa is our signature sanctuary — a bespoke journey that blends contemporary techniques with heritage botanicals sourced from Penang's famous spice routes. Each session is fully personalised by your therapist.

Begin with a warm foot ritual, then surrender to a full-body massage using our house-blended aromatic oils — infused with turmeric, pandan and lemongrass. Close with a cooling herbal compress and face mask.

02 · Malay Heritage

Samporna Spa

Rooted in the ancient healing traditions of the Malay archipelago, Samporna Spa draws from centuries-old Urut Melayu techniques — the rhythmic deep-tissue practice passed down through generations of traditional healers.

Our therapists use warm coconut oil blended with halia (ginger), kunyit (turmeric) and serai (lemongrass) to soothe tired muscles, improve circulation and restore your body's natural energy pathways.

03 · Ancient Chinese

Qin Spa

Named after China's first unified dynasty, Qin Spa is a tribute to 2,000 years of Traditional Chinese Medicine. Our therapists apply Tui Na manipulation, meridian acupressure and reflexology to restore the body's vital Qi flow.

Sessions begin with a detailed wellness consultation. Treatments may include bamboo cupping, heated stone placement and medicated oil application — all calibrated to your constitution and current imbalances.
