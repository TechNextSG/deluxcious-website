Our Story

A Colonial Mansion, Reimagined

Built in the 1880s along Jalan Sultan Ahmad Shah, this Straits Eclectic mansion stood witness to over a century of Penang history. In 2014, we began a five-year restoration that preserved every original timber beam, hand-laid mosaic tile, and colonial archway.

Today, the heritage space is home to Deluxcious Restaurant & Bar — where Chef Keith Khoo's craft meets the architecture of a restored colonial mansion. Situated in the George Town UNESCO World Heritage Site.
