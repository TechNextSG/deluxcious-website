Our Story

A Colonial Mansion, Reimagined

Built in the 1880s along Jalan Sultan Ahmad Shah, this Straits Eclectic mansion stood witness to over a century of Penang history. In 2014, we began a five-year restoration that preserved every original timber beam, hand-laid mosaic tile, and colonial archway.

Today, the building holds 18 private suites, three spa sanctuaries, and a restaurant where Chef Keith Khoo's craft meets the heritage of the space around it. Situated in the George Town UNESCO World Heritage Site.

The Spa

Three Traditions of Healing

Our spa brings together three distinct healing philosophies under one roof — the Deluxcious Signature experience, the Malay heritage tradition of Samporna, and the ancient Chinese meridian practice of Qin.

Each is practiced in a dedicated treatment suite by therapists trained in their respective disciplines. Sessions from RM 160 for 60 minutes.
