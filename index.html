Our Heritage

Where Heritage
Meets Indulgence

Housed in a beautifully restored 19th-century colonial mansion along Jalan Sultan Ahmad Shah, Deluxcious is Penang's most distinctive address — a single destination uniting world-class fusion dining, three bespoke spa experiences, and 18 private heritage suites.

At its heart is our kitchen, led by award-winning Chef Keith Khoo — whose résumé spans Singapore's Ritz-Carlton and the finest tables of the region. Every dish on his menu is a conversation between the world's great cuisines and the vibrant flavours of Penang.

"Expect dishes that will have your taste buds dance with every bite."
