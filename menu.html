Modern Nyonya Cuisine

Tasting Experience

The Heritage Tasting Menu

A seven-course progression that moves from the cool and aromatic to the warm and complex — the full arc of Nyonya flavour in one sitting. Available Tuesday to Sunday, dinner only. Reservation required.

RM 380
Per Person
RM 580
With Wine Pairing
Book Tasting Menu
01
Amuse-Bouche
Pandan coconut gel, toasted kerisik, fresh lime

A one-bite introduction to the evening — the perfume of pandan, the warmth of toasted coconut, and the lift of lime zest. Seasonally adjusted.

02
First Course
Jicama kerabu, torch ginger blossom, prawn floss

Classic Nyonya kerabu reimagined — crisp jicama dressed in our house asam dressing, laid with thread-fine prawn floss and a torch ginger blossom from the courtyard garden.

03
Soup Course
Assam laksa broth, hand-pulled mackerel, penang shrimp paste

A clarified version of Penang's most iconic dish. The broth is made over three days from wild mackerel and lemongrass. Served in a small bowl — enough to open the palate without filling it.

04
Intermezzo
Roselle and calamansi sorbet, sea salt crystal

A palate cleanser using locally grown roselle, pressed to order. The sea salt crystal is hand-harvested from Kuala Sepetang.

05
Main Course
Slow-braised pork belly, Nyonya buah keluak sauce, steamed jasmine rice

Chef Khoo's signature. Pork belly braised for eight hours in dark soy and galangal, served with our house buah keluak sauce — the earthy, smoky seed that defines Peranakan cooking at its most complex.

06
Pre-Dessert
Chilled sago, fresh mango, coconut milk

The familiar comfort of sago pudding, elevated: Harum Manis mango from Balik Pulau, young coconut milk pressed the same morning.

07
Dessert
Ondeh-ondeh, pandan caramel, toasted sesame

A study in the Nyonya dessert tradition. Glutinous rice parcels filled with palm sugar, dusted in toasted coconut, served warm on pandan caramel. Texturally and aromatically, the perfect close.
